me:

My name is Becky Robinson and I'm a front end developer. I have a background in graphic design and everything I touch is colourful. CSS is my jam, I love the challenges of Javascript, and I get so much joy out of making websites that are cute, functional, and accessible to everyone. I am exhilarated by the challenges and rewards that come with coding and how there are always new things to learn and problems to solve. All of the pieces in my portfolio were designed and built by me. I hope you like them as much as I do!
