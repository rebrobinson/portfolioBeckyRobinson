me:

My name is Becky Robinson and I'm a front end developer. I have a background in graphic design and everything I touch is colourful. CSS is my jam, and I love making websites that are cute, functional, and accessible to everyone. I love that as a developer I get to learn so much everyday, and I'm excited to see where this career takes me next. All of the pieces in my portfolio were designed and built by me (with the occasional group member). I hope you like them as much as I do!
